Who We Are

BUILT ON PRECISION.

We specialize in the manufacture of high speed steel (HSS) punches and dowel pins engineered to the tightest tolerances. Every tool we produce goes through rigorous quality checks before it leaves our facility.

From standard catalogued items to fully custom specifications, we serve clients across tooling, die-making, automotive, and engineering sectors.
